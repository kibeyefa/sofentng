A society of entrepreneurs created solely to improve and better the lives of young entrepreneurs

SOFENT is designed to build a community comprising of Prolific thinkers, Content creators, Entrepreneurs, Academicians, Scientists and decision makers from all spheres of life, different backgrounds and cultures. Together, we build the community that will shape the future of Nigeria Youths.
Few of our goals include:

  • Building a community committed to the service of humanity.
  • Building a community where our future will be highly secured.
  • Building a community where each member develops skills to become an international player.

A brief history of SOFENT

Proffering solutions to problems which stand to hunt human and the urge to become one of the most influential entrepreneurial society in history where urgent and permanent solutions will be proffered to imbalances, needs and problems which warrant urgent attentions, spurred three optimists to lay the foundation for SOFENT in 2013. The ideas that did not allow them to sleep, until it became what surfaced and benefited human, made T.C., Ajegbomogun, F.A., Maradesa, A.B., Adegoke and S.O., Adedeji to pioneer this community and laying foundation for what we regarded as SOFENT today. SOFENT was first created at FUT, AKURE by these thinkers. It was later introduced to other students so that the idea of creating own market will become a reality. Today, SOFENT has been expanded to some desired level. To grow SOFENT to the stage that she plays dominant role in entrepreneurial activities in Africa, We thrust her to the second stage of development. And this involves prominent thinker called S.A., Moshood and others. Today, SOFENT comprises four key founders and other members of the community.
