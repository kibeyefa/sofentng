About

Learn More About Us

A society of entrepreneurs created solely to improve and better the lives of young entrepreneurs

A few things we plan on acheiving are:

  • Building a community committed to the service of humanity.
  • Building a community where each member develops skills to become an international player.
  • Building a community where our future will be highly secured.

SOFENT is designed to build a community comprising of Prolific thinkers, Content creators, Entrepreneurs, Academicians, Scientists and decision makers from all spheres of life, different backgrounds and cultures. Together, we build the community that will shape the future of Nigeria Youths.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What Is SOFENT?

    SOFENT is society of entrepreneurs whose aim is to build human capacity and create employment opportunities for young graduates.

  • What problem does SOFENT stand to solve?

    SOFENT is charged with the responsibility of building human capacity and creating employment opportunities for young graduate.

  • Who Can Join SOFENT?

    Anyone who wants to add value to our world, a practicing and aspiring entrepreneur.

  • What is the benefit of being a member?

    Since SOFENT is a fully registered organization, any member can use SOFENT as their affiliation in their business. Every member is a part of SOFENT market and they have all the right to advertise, market in the SOFENT E-commerce platform so as to give the products online visibility and to attract customers across the globe and be hired as a professional in their respective fields.

  • How do I become a memeber.?

    Click here to fill the membership form. You need means of identification, recommenders name and passport photograph.

  • Can Students Join SOFENT?

    Yes.

  • Is SOFENT a registered company?

    Yes, it is fully registered under Corporate Affairs Commission with registration number RC 2713863.
